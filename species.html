Endangered Species

These incredible animals are disappearing fast. Let's protect them before it's too late.

Bengal Tiger

Fact: Bengal tigers have unique stripe patterns like human fingerprints, are powerful swimmers, and can run up to 40 mph for short distances.

Giant Panda

Fact: Giant pandas are fascinating bears with unique adaptations, such as a "false thumb" for gripping bamboo and spending up to 16 hours a day eating it.

Black Rhino

Fact: Black rhinos are solitary and territorial, and their horns are made of keratin, similar to human hair and nails.

Hawksbill Turtle

Fact: Hawksbill turtles are named for their hawk-like beaks, which help them eat sponges and other food from reef crevices.

Orangutan

Fact: They are one of humans' closest relatives, sharing approximately 97% of their DNA, and build elaborate nests for sleeping.

Arctic Fox

Fact: They are also monogamous, have dens that can be centuries old, and can track a seal from a mile away with their keen sense of smell.
